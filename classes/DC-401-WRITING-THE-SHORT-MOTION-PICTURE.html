DC 401 WRITING THE SHORT MOTION PICTURE

Students will study the essential elements and conventions for writing the short film including, character, structure, and tension; they will examine the fundamental differences between a feature script and short form. Students will write a polished short film designed to be produced as the student's MFA thesis or other route of production.
