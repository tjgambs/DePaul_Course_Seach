EA 537 REFLECTIVE PRACTICE SEMINAR III

At the end of quarter six, this seminar offers students an opportunity to get feedback on plans for the Applied Inquiry Project, to conduct an informal mid-program review, to examine ways in which students are applying the three Elements of Better Practice, to identify significant threads across the curriculum, and present on aspects of their program portfolios. PREREQUISITE(S): Status as an MAEA student or departmental permission is a prerequisite for this class.
