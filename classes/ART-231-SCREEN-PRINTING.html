ART 231 SCREEN PRINTING

A beginning level course designed to teach students the essential equipment, materials, and processes, for screen printing artworks. Printing methods utilize hand-cut stencils, screen filler, and photo-emulsion techniques. ART 101 or ART 105 recommended.
