UIP 240 CAREER PATHWAYS: EXPLORE CAREER OPTIONS

This 2-credit course will help you analyze your values, skills, personality preferences and studies as a foundation for career possibilities. You will meet with a mentor from the Chicago community, who shares your career interests, and you will examine a variety of career paths, supported by research and personal reflection.
