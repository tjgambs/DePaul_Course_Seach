DC 323 PRE-PRODUCTION FOR CINEMA

This course will cover the pre-production phase of short and feature-length filmmaking, including fundraising, breaking down scripts, scheduling, budgeting and pre-visualizing. Professional scheduling, budgeting and pre-visualization software will be utilized. Additionally, the roles of the creative producer, line producer and assistant director will be examined. Prerequisite(s): DC 210
