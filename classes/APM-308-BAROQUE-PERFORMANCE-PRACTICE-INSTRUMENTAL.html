APM 308 BAROQUE PERFORMANCE PRACTICE-INSTRUMENTAL

This course focuses on the interpretation and performance of music from the Baroque era, concentrating primarily on 18th century literature. Open to all instrumentalists, students will study Baroque performance practice based on the treatises and various writings of the time, as well as current scholarly research of early music specialists.
