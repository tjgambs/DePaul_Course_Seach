ACC 374 AUDITING II

Auditing II, a companion and sequel to ACC 372, emphasizes the implementation and application of generally accepted auditing standards to transaction cycles and classes of transactions. It includes the study of sampling techniques used in tests of controls and tests of details, internal control in computer-based systems.
