ART 323 INTERMEDIATE PHOTOGRAPHY

This course continues the aesthetic development and processes of film-based, black & white photography through a refinement of techniques and conceptual practices. Large and medium-format cameras are introduced along with fiber-based printing, additional lighting and exposure strategies, and darkroom print finishing techniques. Emphasis is on developing and enhancing critical approaches to subject matter through project-based, hands-on experimentation and critical discussion.
