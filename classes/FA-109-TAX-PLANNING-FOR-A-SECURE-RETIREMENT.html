FA 109 TAX PLANNING FOR A SECURE RETIREMENT

The Internal Revenue Service (IRS) administers tax laws which affect retirement income. What investment options are sheltered against taxes? What are some of the growth funds that have historically kept pace with economic growth in spite of taxes? These are some of the questions which will be addressed in this course. Students will learn to describe, differentiate, and explain form, function, and variation within functional business processes. More specifically, this course examines: 1. Social security, medicare, defined benefit plans and defined contribution plans. 2. Quantitative methods to determine the amount of retirement money you may need in the future.
