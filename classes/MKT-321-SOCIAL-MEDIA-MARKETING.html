MKT 321 SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING

In this course students gain a better understanding of what social media is and how businesses and individuals are using it to build awareness, increase visibility, and engage in conversations with their customers. Students also gain an understanding of the various benefits of social media, and insight into the relatively low cost and high scalability.
