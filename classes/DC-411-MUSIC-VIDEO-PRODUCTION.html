DC 411 MUSIC VIDEO PRODUCTION

In this course, we will analyze ways in which artists combine visual imagery with music as with MTV-style music videos. The music business and how it relates/effects music videos. Each student will develop his or hew own music video project from script to final edit with a local band of their choice, through their own scheduling process. PREREQUISITE(S): (DC 210 and DC 220) or DC 460 or DC 409
