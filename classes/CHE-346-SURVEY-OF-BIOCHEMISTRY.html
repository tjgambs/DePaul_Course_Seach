CHE 346 SURVEY OF BIOCHEMISTRY

This course covers the key topics in biochemistry. These include non-covalent interactions, acid/base chemistry, chemical and three-dimensional structures of proteins/nucleic acids/lipids, hemoglobin oxygen binding, cytoskeletal and motor proteins, enzyme mechanisms/kinetics, membrane and membrane proteins/transport, signaling, energy metabolism and biosynthesis. Formerly CHE 240.
