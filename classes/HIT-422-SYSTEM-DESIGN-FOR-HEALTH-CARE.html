HIT 422 SYSTEM DESIGN FOR HEALTH CARE

Course focus is on systems design for health care that enhances quality of patient care and satisfaction, staff efficiency, and improving patient outcomes. Specific topics include: database design; interface and dialogue design in the context of workflow; usability and human factors; report design; system implementation; and system maintenance. PREREQUISITE(S): IS 421 and CSC 451
