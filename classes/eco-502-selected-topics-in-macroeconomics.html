ECO 502 SELECTED TOPICS IN MACROECONOMICS

This brief two credit hour course in macroeconomics will cover the nature of macroeconomic activity including the component parts of aggregate expenditures and the nature of key indicators of the economy's performance. The roles of fiscal and monetary policy makers will be examined as well as the structure of the central bank.
