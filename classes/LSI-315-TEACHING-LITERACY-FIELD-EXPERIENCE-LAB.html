LSI 315 TEACHING LITERACY FIELD EXPERIENCE LAB

In this Lab, taken with LSI 329, candidates provide reading and writing instruction to exceptional students and use informal assessments and progress monitoring to guide instructional practices and differentiation. Supervision, coaching, and mentoring in implementing effective instructional strategies for exceptional students are provided by program faculty. The course develops the concept of documenting impact on student learning. This experience is offered in an on-campus facility that serves at-risk literacy learners and students with disabilities. COREQUISITE: LSI 329.
