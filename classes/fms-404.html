FMS 404 INTERNATIONAL CONFLICT MANAGEMENT

The course focuses on theoretical foundations and practical implications of diplomacy, negotiations, meditation and peace building to solve and prevent conflicts. It offers reflective practices and concrete directions for creating constructive solutions to interpersonal, and inter-group, and international conflict. Cross-listed with MPS 616.
