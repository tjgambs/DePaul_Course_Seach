ACC 635 PRINCIPLES OF FORENSIC ACCOUNTING

This course will focus on fraud principles that relate to asset misappropriations, corruption and fraudulent financial statements. Emphasis will be on examination and review of major fraud schemes, investigative strategies and controls used to detect and prevent the impact fraud has on an organization.
