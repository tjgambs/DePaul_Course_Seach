FIN 540 CORPORATE GOVERNANCE

This course addresses the overall role that internal audit plays as a critical part of an organization's risk control and governance structure. Internal risk management systems and metrics are examined in detail. Compliance with these risk parameters are examined from the perspective of the Chief Risk officer, the Board of Directors, and the Chief Executive Officer.
