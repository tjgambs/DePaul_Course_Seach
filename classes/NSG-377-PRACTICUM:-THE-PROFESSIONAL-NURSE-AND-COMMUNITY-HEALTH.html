NSG 377 PRACTICUM: THE PROFESSIONAL NURSE AND COMMUNITY HEALTH

This provides opportunities for developing the professional nurse role in community health care. Experiential learning activities focus on community assessment, health program planning and evaluation, and management of innovation at the community level. Health education and leadership development are emphasized in both public health and community-based settings involved in health care delivery for individuals, families, and communities.
