MED 401 SEMINAR IN MUSIC EDUCATION I: PHILOSOPHY OF MUSIC EDUCATION

The courses are designed 1) to develop a philosophical and historical perspective of music education; 2) to study the psychology of music; 3) to study topics in curriculum design; 4) to encourage study and research in areas of student interest and need; and 5) to study contemporary issues in music education that can have significant impact on classroom teaching.
