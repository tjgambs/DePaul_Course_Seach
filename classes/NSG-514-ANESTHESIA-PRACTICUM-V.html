NSG 514 ANESTHESIA PRACTICUM V

The second in a series of advanced practicums that provide the third year nurse anesthesia resident (NAR) with the opportunity to develop, implement, and evaluate an anesthesia care plan for all patient populations undergoing a wide variety of surgical and therapeutic procedures. Emphasis is placed on application, integration, and synthesis of knowledge, skills, and attitudes that are representative of the advanced practice of nurse anesthesia. Includes seminar presentations on selected topics and issues relative to the advanced practice of anesthesia and completion of online bimonthly review quizzes.
