NSG 330 FOUNDATIONS OF PROFESSIONAL NURSING PRACTICE

This course introduces the registered nurse student to the philosophy and mission of the DePaul Department of Nursing University Program. The central focus will be on the evaluation and analysis of the associate degree nurse transition to educator and leadership management roles at the advanced degree level. Major areas of emphasis will include the evolution of nursing as a scientific professional discipline, the role of the professional nurse as a client advocate, change agent as an educator and leader, the role of power and politics as sources of empowerment, legal and ethical parameters in the scope of nursing practice.
