GD 356 LOGO, BRANDING, AND VISUAL IDENTITY

The course provides students with an understanding of methodologies and strategies used for logo development, branding, and visual identity. In this course, students will work on, in extended projects, exploring identity systems with various forms such as logo, stationary, packaging, signage, print and web design. PREREQUISITE(S): GD 200 and GD 230
