FIN 500 UNIVERSITY EXPERIENCE: ACADEMIC AND BUSINESS CULTURE

This course provides students with an understanding of how to be successful in the United States academically and professionally. The course covers concepts, methods, and tools necessary to face the challenges of studying and working in the U.S., paying particular attention to unique challenges faced by international students from Asia and other regions of the world.
