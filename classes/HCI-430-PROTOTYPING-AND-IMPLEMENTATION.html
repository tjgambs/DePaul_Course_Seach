HCI 430 PROTOTYPING AND IMPLEMENTATION

Overview of prototyping methods ranging from low-fidelity to interactive script-based prototypes. Analysis of prototyping tools and their relative advantages. Projects include prototype development over multiple iterations using a variety of strategies. PREREQUISITE(S): IT 411. Students must have completed or be concurrently enrolled in HCI 440 or HCI 441 to register for this course.
