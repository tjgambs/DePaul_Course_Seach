HAA 130 EUROPEAN ART

This introductory-level course examines the art historical methods and their application to a broad chronological and select survey of European art and architecture from pre-history to the 20th century. Field trips to Chicago art institutions extend the visual traditions and critical methods taught in class lectures and discussions.
