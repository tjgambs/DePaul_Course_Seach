ENV 294 SECOND YEAR SEMINAR

The purpose of this course is to advance ecological literacy. The seminar presents the study of the environment to students in a philosophical, cultural, and historical context, and in addition makes them aware of some foundational ideas of the discipline through a selection of new and classic literature.
