FA 260 ESSENTIALS OF PROJECT MANAGEMENT

This course provides an overview of the fundamentals of Project Management for non-Project Managers. It introduces the four phases of the project management process: Initiating, Planning, Managing, and Closing; the role of the Project Manager; tools, techniques and deliverables associated with successful project management; and troubleshooting techniques.
