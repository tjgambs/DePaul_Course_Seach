SUD 501 PROFESSIONAL INTERNSHIP

A 100-hr internship with one of the following: a local government, non-profit organization, or private corporation. Students are encouraged to work onsite, integrating their learning into a Chicago-area organization. Students unable to identify an internship will be assisted by the Steans Center for Community-based Service Learning, the Chaddick Institute, and other DePaul resources.
